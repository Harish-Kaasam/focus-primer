# User Guide

Focus Primer is designed to prepare your nervous system for deep work.

## The 4 Focus Phases

### 1. Session Setup
Adjust the settings to customize your flow:
- **Fixation duration**: Gaze warmup duration.
- **Focus duration**: Your main focus block.
- **Include Ujjayi breathing**: Switch ON to include a breathing practice first.

### 2. Fixation Phase
Concentrate on the white/blue center point inside the rotating concentric rings. Keep your gaze static.

### 3. Ujjayi Breathing Phase
Follow the expansion and contraction of the teal breathing orb:
- **Inhale**: Breathe in slowly through your nose as the orb expands.
- **Exhale**: Breathe out slowly through your nose as the orb contracts.

### 4. Focus Session
Focus entirely on your target task. The blue ring progress indicator will deplete clockwise. You can check your stats (current daily streaks) directly below the timer.
